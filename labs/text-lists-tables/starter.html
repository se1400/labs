Welcome to Utah Tech

Utah Tech University is located in St. George, Utah. Our campus sits at the base of beautiful red rock cliffs in Southern Utah.

We are committed to active learning and helping students succeed.

With 300+ days of sunshine per year and a 20:1 student-faculty ratio, Utah Tech offers an ideal learning environment.

Why Choose Utah Tech?

Small class sizes with hands-on learning.

Over 200 programs to choose from.

Beautiful weather and outdoor adventures.
