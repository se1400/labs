Welcome to Utah Tech

Utah Tech University is located in St. George, Utah. Our campus sits at the base of beautiful red rock cliffs in Southern Utah.

We are committed to active learning and helping students succeed.

With 300+ degrees of sunshine per year and a 20:1 student-faculty ratio1, Utah Tech offers an ideal learning environment.
